
# Intro

I completed my Master's in Computer Science at the [University of São Paulo](https://www.ime.usp.br/pos-computacao/) in 2025, with research focused on developing computer vision methods for resource-constrained devices.


# A bit of my story

- I first encountered a computer in 2009 at my aunt's house. By Christmas 2010, my parents managed to get me one — that year I had left my hometown to study at a military school in the state capital.

- There I completed a technical course in computer networking, where I learned more about computers and discovered that my favorite operating system was Linux.

- After high school, I set out to study robotics because I loved it, so I took a vocational technical course in mechatronics and learned how to build robots :).

- I needed to go to university and there was no robotics program in my city, so I chose computer science — where I tried to focus on the robotics groups but discovered I loved mathematics and that studying artificial intelligence (AI) was possible there.

- Since then I studied AI, optimization, image processing, and wrote a lot of code.

- In 2019 I published 3 papers on my undergraduate work: 2 in domestic conferences and 1 in an international journal, which also became my final thesis.

- I have always liked teaching because I learned so much by doing it — and thanks to that I landed my first job, as a course instructor.

- I then started working as a developer and in 2020 I moved from Tocantins to São Paulo, a big achievement in my life.

- In 2021 my girlfriend, who had been living in Tocantins, came to live with me and we adopted a little dog together :).

- In 2022 I started my master's degree in computer science — something I had long wanted but needed to get organized to manage working and studying at the same time.

- In 2023 I had incredible experiences at companies like Ingredion and Mantis-AI, expanding my expertise in data science and computer vision.

- In 2024/2025 I completed my master's at USP and took on the role of Machine Learning Engineer at Venturus in Manaus, bringing research and AI to products with real impact.
