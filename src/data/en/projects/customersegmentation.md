#### Background

The client company was already active in software development and was starting to build a data team and shift its internal culture. One of the key needs was greater predictability of projects and, above all, increasing project sales.

#### The Problem

The sales team was operating with little data-driven insight about clients and needed strategies to forecast which contracts were likely to close, and to treat each client individually in order to increase conversion rates.

#### The Solution

Using CRM data that mapped the customer journey, we analyzed each client and grouped them by similarity. These groups essentially separated clients into different levels of conversion probability. After the segmentation, a study was conducted to analyze why some clients closed deals and others did not.

#### Results

With a better-informed sales team, they were able to give the appropriate treatment to each type of client based on their needs. This had a direct impact on the conversion rate and on NPS, since the segmentation solution was adopted by the CX team. Project pipeline predictability also improved, giving leadership greater capacity to plan resource allocation.

**Stacks:** Python, TensorFlow, scikit-image, OpenCV, gdal, rasterio, scikit-learn, AWS (s3, ec2).
